About

Sidharth Sriram (born 19 May 1990[1]) is an Indian Carnatic musician, music producer, playback singer, and songwriter raised in America. He is an R&B songwriter and has been working in the Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Hindi, Marathi and English music industry.He regularly collaborates with his sister Pallavi Sriram, a Bharatanatyam dancer, and music directors
